Hopegrown Productions is a Montreal-based theatre company, founded in Fall 2012 by Miriam Cummings, Lindsey Huebner and Samantha Megarry. We are committed to staging relevant stories featuring dynamic female characters.

In 2013, Hopegrown’s debut production Around Miss Julie by Harry Standjofksi completed an ambitious 6-city tour, including the Edinburgh Festival Fringe. Exciting things to come in 2014! Check back in January!
