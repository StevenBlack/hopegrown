Our 2014 production: Lotus

by Caitlin Murphy

Director: Bryan Doubt
Designer: Sonya Vallis

Featuring:

Miriam Cummings
Samantha Megarry

When a gruesome picture appears on a teenager’s facebook page, a young police officer becomes intent on proving that something heinous has occurred.

But is it a crime or a prank? Is the teen a silenced victim of bullying or a savvy negotiator in a culture of ambiguity?

Lotus will premiere at the St-Ambroise Montreal Fringe in June 2014. stay tuned for more info!
