A Nightmare For Oberon by Julie Foster

Shakespeare's women have gone off-script and need a girls' night... A drowning goes awry and Ophelia lands in the faerie kingdom with a chain smoking Titania and an obsessive Lady Macbeth. Magic, love, and drinking ensue. Lots and lots of drinking.
